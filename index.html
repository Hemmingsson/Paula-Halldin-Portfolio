Hi. I’m Paula. A Swedish copywriter based in Berlin. I currently work as a junior copywriter at DCMN after two years of studies at Miami Ad School and experiences in both New York ♪, London,♪ and Berlin♪. Feel free to dig through my memories. I usually collect them into playlists. I love coming up with digital ideas and my favorite pieces are for Apple and Amnesty as well as some print for UN Women and Happy Socks. Apart from the advertising world, I also studied fashion for a year. I like fashion. Not as much as I like music though. I always thought I’d become that big star but the lyrics turned into copy and today I’m just a great storyteller. Find me on Pinterest, Instagram, and Soundcloud. Or just leave me a message - I like coffee and random meetings. For further information, download my resumé.

Hush Sounds

Hush Sounds is an extension to Apple Music that translates the melody of a song into a bass line. Making it possible for everyone to enjoy music. Even if you can't hear it.
