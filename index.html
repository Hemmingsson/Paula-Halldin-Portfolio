Hi. I’m Paula. A copywriter born and raised in Stockholm. I just graduated from Miami Ad School after two years of studies including experiences at agencies such as JWT New York♪, McCann London♪ and BBDO Berlin♪. Feel free to dig my memories. I usually collect them into playlists. I love coming up with digital ideas and my favorite pieces are for Apple, The Telegraph, Xbox and Grand Thef Auto as well as Unicef. And some prints for UN Women and Happy Socks. Apart from the advertising world, I also studied fashion for a year. I like fashion. Not as much as I like music though. I always thought I’d become that big star but the lyrics turned into copy and today I’m just a great storyteller. Find me on Pinterest, Instagram, and Soundcloud. Or just leave me a message - I like coffee and random meetings. For further information, download my resumé.
